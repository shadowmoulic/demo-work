01 / Data Collection

Hustle House is a professional engineering portfolio. We do not automatically collect personal identification information from our visitors. We only receive information that you voluntarily provide when contacting us via WhatsApp, Cal.com, or LinkedIn.

02 / Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with trusted third-party services to facilitate communication and scheduling:

  • **WhatsApp**: For direct messaging and lead generation.
  • **Cal.com**: For scheduling strategy sessions.
  • **LinkedIn**: For professional networking and identity verification.

Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these providers for information on how they handle your data.

03 / Analytics & Cookies

We may utilize lightweight analytics tools to understand site traffic and optimize performance. These tools do not track individual users or collect PII. We use essential session cookies only for technical functionality (e.g., animation states and layout responsiveness).

04 / Data Security

As an engineering-first house, we take digital security seriously. All interactions are handled over encrypted channels (HTTPS), and we ensure that any business-sensitive information shared during strategy calls is treated with the highest level of confidentiality.
