Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students need to know before joining the NV family.

How does NV Classes adapt to different learning paces?

Based on our internal diagnostic assessments, we create customized peer groups of students with similar learning needs and paces. This ensures fast learners remain challenged while foundational learners receive the patience and support required to master concepts without feeling overwhelmed.

What is the typical class size, and how do you ensure personalized attention?

We strictly cap our batch sizes at 20 to 25 students maximum. This intimate ratio enables our faculty to know every student personally, track daily performance, and conduct targeted one-on-one doubt clearing sessions.

What resources and study materials do you provide?

We provide exhaustive concept modules, daily practice problem (DPP) sheets, weekly All-India test series, detailed video solutions, and exclusive access to our on-campus Study Hub & Doubts Clinic for distraction-free preparation.

How do you track and communicate our child's progress with parents?

We conduct regular Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs) and send transparent weekly performance reports via WhatsApp and Email. You will always be informed about test scores, attendance, and specific focus areas.

Uncompromising Academic Integrity

We do not make false promises. We commit to unwavering hard work, elite faculty mentorship, and creating a supportive ecosystem where your child can thrive academically and personally.
