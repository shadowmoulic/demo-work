Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students need to know before joining Bangalore's top NEET & CET institute.

What makes EXCEL ACADEMICS the best NEET coaching institute for repeaters?

Our Repeater/Re-NEET batch provides an intensive, highly focused environment with daily mock tests, individual doubt clearance booths, NCERT line-by-line master classes, and 24x7 library access specifically structured for second-attempt success.

How does the PU Integrated NEET / KCET program work?

The PU Integrated program perfectly synchronizes 1st and 2nd PUC state board curriculum with high-level competitive NEET and KCET preparation during regular college hours. This eliminates double tuition fatigue and gives students ample self-study time.

Are separate hostel facilities available for outstation students?

Yes! We provide fully secure, clean, and supervised separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls located within short walking distance of our academic campus in Yelahanka New Town, complete with hygienic dining and study rooms.

How can parents track their child's academic progress?

We maintain 100% transparency. Parents receive weekly test score updates via SMS and WhatsApp, attendance tracking alerts, and are invited to regular one-on-one Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs) with academic directors.
