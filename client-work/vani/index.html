Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your preparation journey with Vani Institute.

What are the upcoming batch start dates for GATE & ESE 2027/28?
Our new fresh offline and online batches for GATE & ESE 2027/28 commence on April 4th & April 11th. Admissions are currently open across all our centers in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, Kochi, and Kolkata.
How do I book a free demo class and counseling session?
You can easily book your free 1-on-1 counseling session or demo class by filling out the quick inquiry form at the top of this page. Once submitted, our senior academic counselor will call you within 15 minutes to confirm your preferred slot.
Are safe hostel and PG accommodations available near classroom centers?
Yes! We maintain a curated directory of verified, secure, and hygienic hostels/PGs (separate for boys and girls) within walking distance of all our physical coaching centers. Our administration team actively assists parents and students with accommodation setup.
Do you offer scholarships or fee concessions for meritorious students?
Yes, we conduct regular National Scholarship Tests where deserving candidates can secure up to 50% tuition fee waivers based on their academic merit and test performance. Special fee concessions are also available for university toppers and alumni referrals.
Can I upgrade from Online Live classes to Offline Classroom coaching later?
Absolutely. We provide complete flexibility. If you start your preparation online and later wish to relocate to any of our physical centers (such as Hyderabad or Bangalore), your paid fees will be seamlessly adjusted toward the offline classroom program.
