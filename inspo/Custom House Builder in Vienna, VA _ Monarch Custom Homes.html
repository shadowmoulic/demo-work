About Monarch Custom Homes

We Can’t Wait To Make Your Ideas A Reality

Monarch Construction is a full-service custom home builder based in the Washington D.C. Metropolitan suburb of Vienna serving the communities of Northern Virginia. Creating the custom home of your dreams requires a tremendous amount of forethought and effort.

Architectural design, engineering, permitting, selections, and construction for an exceptional custom home can be overwhelming for most people. That is why Monarch is more than a custom builder.

We offer all these services under one roof, to ensure that your home is delivered on time, within budget, and at a level of craftsmanship unmatched in this area.

The Monarch Process

1. Pre-construction Planning & Permits

Our design engineers engage early in the design and pre-construction planning process to work within your budget to build a home that not only meets but exceeds your expectations. You will go over every detail of your home with the team who will be taking your vision and making it a reality. 

2. Site Preparation and Break Ground

There’s nothing more groundbreaking than actually breaking ground on your dream home. From that moment forward, our team will keep you informed throughout the construction process. 

3. Construction Management & Execution

We worry about all the details so that you don’t have to. With our experienced management and skilled construction staff, we will ensure your project completes on time and within budget.
